## Project & Team Management

**JIRA** — Sprint planning, bug tracking, epic management. The source of truth for everything the squad is working on.

**Confluence** — Documentation. QA artifacts, runbooks, post-mortem records. If it's not written down, it didn't happen.

**GitLab** — Version control, CI/CD pipelines, and merge request tracking. We run all our automation gates here.

**Slack** — Team communication. I'm a big advocate for async-first communication with clear escalation paths for urgent issues.

## Engineering & Automation

**Appium** — The backbone of our mobile automation suite. Cross-platform coverage for Android and iOS from a single codebase.

**Python** — My preferred language for automation scripting. Readable, fast to iterate, and the team can all contribute.

**Postman** — API testing and documentation. Combined with JMeter for performance and load scenarios.

**BrowserStack** — Real device testing. Non-negotiable for mobile automation in a market with diverse device fragmentation.

**JMeter** — Load and performance testing. Essential for validating the platform before major events (Eid, promotions, etc.).

## AI & Productivity

**GitHub Copilot** — AI-assisted coding. Particularly useful for boilerplate and test case generation first drafts.

**Claude / ChatGPT** — For writing, analysis, and thought partnership on engineering decisions. I treat LLMs as a thinking partner, not an answer machine.

**Notion** — Personal knowledge management and writing drafts.

## Leadership Tools

**Loom** — Async video updates for stakeholders. Much more efficient than scheduling a call for status updates.

**Linear** — When I need lightweight issue tracking outside JIRA's enterprise overhead.

**Miro** — Online whiteboarding for architecture discussions and retrospectives.

## Learning

**ISTQB Materials** — Always have the Foundation and Advanced Level materials bookmarked. Quality principles age well.

**Y Combinator Startup School** — The Startup School 2022 curriculum shaped how I think about building and scaling products.

**LinkedIn Learning** — The Product Masterclass series and leadership courses I've completed are listed on my profile.
