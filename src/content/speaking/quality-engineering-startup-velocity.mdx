A community meetup talk organized as part of the Product Soch Cafe series at Bykea. Covered the practical challenges of quality engineering in a high-velocity startup — from building the first automation framework to transforming how the whole engineering org thinks about quality.

Key topics covered:
- The "QA as gatekeeper" anti-pattern and why it kills delivery speed
- How we built Appium-based automation covering 3 apps with one codebase
- The 3 cultural changes that actually moved the needle
- Metrics to track quality improvement (not just test counts)
