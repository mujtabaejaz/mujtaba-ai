Internal engineering presentation covering Bykea's pilot program for AI-augmented testing workflows. Presented findings from a 2-squad, 4-sprint pilot including:

- Where AI-generated test cases saved time (first-draft generation from acceptance criteria)
- Where AI added noise (flaky scenario generation for complex business logic)
- The review workflow that kept humans accountable for AI outputs
- Metrics: 40% faster test planning, with quality maintained

The presentation led to org-wide adoption of AI-assisted tooling across all three engineering squads.
