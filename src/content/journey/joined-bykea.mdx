## The Culture Shift

Softech was methodical. Bykea was on fire — in the best way. Releases happened weekly. Features shipped fast. User counts were climbing. And the QA process hadn't caught up.

I had to rewire my thinking: in a startup, **speed and quality are not opposites — bad quality is what kills speed.**

## What I Built in Year One

- Signed off on releases impacting 3M+ users across 3 apps
- Maintained 99.5% production build stability
- Introduced structured defect policies and automation foundations

The real learning was how to prioritize: not all bugs matter equally, and knowing which ones block a release versus which ones can wait is judgment that only comes from being close to the user.
