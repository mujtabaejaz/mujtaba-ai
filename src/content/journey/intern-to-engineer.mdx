## Starting Point

Joined Softech Worldwide as an intern in February 2018, working on VIDIZMO — an enterprise video streaming platform used by Fortune 500 companies. My job was to write TypeScript automation scripts.

What I learned: **automation without strategy is just more code to maintain.** Good test design requires understanding the user journey deeply.

Earned "Trainer of the Month" recognition in my first year for organizing internal training sessions. Even then, I understood that sharing knowledge multiplies impact.
