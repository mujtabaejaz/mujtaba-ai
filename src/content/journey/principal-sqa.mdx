## The Scope Change

Principal SQA meant I was no longer just doing QA — I was designing how QA worked across the entire platform. That included:

- Planning and prioritizing all testing activities across features
- Owning the release sign-off process for all three apps
- Leading daily scrums and sprint ceremonies
- Managing the QA team's hiring, onboarding, and growth

The biggest learning: **communication is a core engineering skill**, not a soft skill add-on. Demoing features to stakeholders, managing ETAs, escalating risks — these mattered as much as the technical work.

## The ISTQB Initiative

Introduced mandatory ISTQB Foundation certification for the entire QA team. The result wasn't just better test coverage — it was a shared professional identity. The team started seeing themselves as quality engineers, not testers.
